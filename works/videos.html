THE START OF A GANGSTER

A silent film I edited for my history class in my senior year in high school

My history teacher gave all of us the task to develop a silent film that reflected on a topic that was significant in the 1930s. My friend, Adam Marchwinski, assembled a group of 6 (myself included) and chose the topic of extreme poverty and the rise of the Chicago mafia.
